London Boutique

Aoyama Flower Market was established in 1989 in the exclusive Aoyama district of Tokyo,
and has opened its 100th store in Japan in 2018.

After opening our first overseas shop in 2015 on the Rue du Bac in Paris,
we are now in Selfridges London, since May 2018. We are located at the heart of Central London, where all the new trends and fashion are made.

Our aim is to create a fulfilling moment with the freshest flowers and plants, and deliver it to Londoners and every visitors of our shop.

Located in the heart of London, our shop is easily accessible. The closest station is Bond Street Station. There are several bus stops on Orchard Street and Oxford Street. If you need further help to plan your journey to our shop, you can call us or look at the Google Maps.
